About MONTE

Mobility & Optimized Network for Transit Efficiency

This application is part of a student research project at City of San Jose del Monte National Science High School.

Objective: To analyze traffic volume and provide commuters with data-driven fare estimates and terminal locating services.

"Quantifying Traffic Congestion and Enhancing Commuter Efficiency through Geographic Information Systems."
